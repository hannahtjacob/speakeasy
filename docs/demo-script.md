# Demo Script

## 0:00-0:20 Hook

Slack messages can pull a driver's eyes to a phone. SpeakEasy turns important Slack activity into spoken updates.

## 0:20-0:50 Setup

Show the Slack workspace, the SpeakEasy companion page, and the backend running through ngrok.

## 0:50-1:30 Speak Alerts

Run:

```text
/speak-alerts on
```

Show Slack confirming alerts are on.

## 1:30-2:10 Spoken Summary

Send a message in Slack. Show the backend receiving the event, Groq creating a short summary, and the companion page reading it aloud.

## 2:10-2:35 Toggle Off

Run:

```text
/speak-alerts off
```

Show that the preference updates.

## 2:35-3:00 Impact

Explain that SpeakEasy helps drivers, people with visual impairments, people with limited hand mobility, and hands-busy workers stay connected without looking at a screen.
